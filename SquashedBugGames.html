About

Game Development News

So you're a gamer; thanks for visiting my page. I've created this site to post updates on my current game projects and blogs. If you're anything like me, you love games of all genres. I've been gaming since I can remember. I was born in 1981, when I was 3 years old, my father brought home a Texas Instruments TI-99. My first games that I remember playing were Alpiner, Munch Man, and Car Wars. Now, I'm 37 and studying Game Development.

ABOUT

Squashed Bug Games is a derivitive of the Eagle Rising Insignia from the Warrant Officer Corps. I've been a Warrant Officer in the US Army since 2014 serving as an Information Systems Technician.

LOCATION

Colorado Springs is where my time in game development took off. This is where my family and I plan to stay for the indefinite future. Beautiful Scenery and interesting weather are no strangers to this land.
