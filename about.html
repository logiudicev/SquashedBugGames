about Pasta Amore

Pasta Amore originated in Cicily Italy in 1921; the owner, Vinny Renzo Luigi immigrated to the United States in 1930 and fought in World War II. He told me he would open the Restaurant if he ever made it back from the war alive. Well that day came! In 1946, Vinny opened the resaurant and brought his whole family in to help run it. Fast forward to 2020, the restaurant is still going strong with all the original secret recipes Vinny made. Vinny passed away in 2011 at 100 years old; he had a great life and would be proud of what the restaurant has achieved since then.
